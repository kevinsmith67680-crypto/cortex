Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 June 2026

This policy explains what information the Cortex app ("the app", "we", "us") collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. The app is provided by Cortex. If you have questions, contact us at Eddie.kung1@icloud.com.

Our approach is to keep as much as possible on your own device, and to send off your device only what is needed to generate a quiz or — if you choose to create an account — to sync your progress across devices.

Information stored only on your device

By default, the following are stored locally on your device (in the app's local storage) and are not sent to us:

  • Your XP, ranks, and per-book grades
  • Your library of quizzed books and your score history
  • Your daily streak and any achievements
  • Notes you save in the app
  • Your settings (theme, quiz length) and the record of which questions you have already been asked

If you never create an account, this information stays on your device. Deleting the app, or using "Reset progress" in Settings, removes it.

Information sent to generate a quiz

When you generate a quiz, the app sends to our server, and our server sends on to our quiz-generation provider Anthropic (the Claude API), the following:

  • In book mode: the book's title and, where available, author and year.
  • In notes mode: the text of the notes you paste.
  • A short list of recently-asked questions, so new quizzes don't repeat them.

In book mode, the title may also be used to perform web searches to ground the questions in accurate detail. Anthropic processes this content to generate questions and returns them to you. See Anthropic's privacy and data-usage terms at anthropic.com/legal/privacy. We do not use your notes or titles for advertising, and we do not sell them.

Book search

When you search for a book, your search text is sent to the Open Library service (operated by the Internet Archive) to return matching titles. See their terms.

Accounts and cloud sync (optional)

If you choose to create an account to sync your progress across devices, we use Supabase for authentication and storage, and you can sign in with Sign in with Apple or Google. In that case we receive and store:

  • An account identifier and the email address associated with your sign-in
  • Your quiz history (book titles, authors, scores), grades, and XP
  • Time spent in the app, used to show your own activity back to you

This data is linked to your account so it can sync across your devices. It is used only to operate the app's features — not for advertising or tracking across other apps or websites.

If you use the app without signing in, none of the above account data is collected, and your progress stays on your device.

Technical information

When your device contacts our server, the server briefly processes your IP address to enforce rate limits and prevent abuse. It is not used to identify you and is not retained beyond what is needed for that purpose. Our hosting provider is Render. Fonts are delivered by Google Fonts when the app loads.

What we do not do

  • We do not show advertising.
  • We do not use third-party analytics or tracking SDKs.
  • We do not sell or rent your personal information.
  • We do not track you across other companies' apps or websites.

Your consent, including AI processing

When you generate a quiz, you are asking us to send the book title or the notes you provide to our AI quiz-generation provider, Anthropic, so it can create your questions. By starting a quiz you consent to that processing. You can decline at any time by not generating a quiz, and you control what you type or paste. If you create an account, you also consent to the storage and syncing described above. You may withdraw consent by signing out, deleting your data, or no longer using the relevant feature.

Sharing your data and third parties

We share data only with the service providers needed to run the app: Anthropic (AI quiz generation), Open Library / the Internet Archive (book search), Supabase (accounts and storage, if you sign in), Apple and Google (sign-in, if you use it), Render (hosting), and Google Fonts (font delivery). We do not sell your data or share it with advertisers or data brokers. Where we share your data with a third party, we require that they protect it to a standard at least equal to that described in this policy and use it only to provide their service to us.

How we keep your data safe

All data sent between the app, our server, and our providers travels over encrypted HTTPS connections. Your Anthropic API key and any server secrets are stored as server environment variables, never in the app. Access to any account data we store is restricted. No method of transmission or storage is ever completely secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

How long we keep your data

  • On-device data (XP, library, streak, saved notes, settings) stays on your device until you reset progress or delete the app.
  • Account data (email, account identifier, quiz history, grades, XP, session time) is kept while your account exists and is deleted when you delete your data or account, except where we must retain limited information for security, fraud prevention, or legal reasons.
  • Book titles and notes sent to generate a quiz are processed to create your questions and are not stored by us afterwards; their handling by Anthropic is governed by Anthropic's terms.
  • IP addresses are processed transiently for rate limiting and are not stored long-term.

Your choices and how to delete your data

  • On-device data: use "Reset progress" in Settings, or delete the app.
  • Cloud data: if you have an account, use the data-deletion control in Settings to remove your synced quiz history, grades, and XP from our servers.
  • Account and data deletion: to delete your account and all associated data, email us at Eddie.kung1@icloud.com with the subject "Delete my Cortex account". We will delete your account and associated data, other than anything we must keep for security, fraud prevention, or legal reasons. This page is also the place to request deletion if you installed Cortex from an app store.

Children

The app is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

Data location and your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or receive a copy of your personal information (data portability), or to object to certain processing. To exercise them, contact Eddie.kung1@icloud.com. Our services and providers may process and store data in the United States and other countries, and your data may be transferred there.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will revise the "Last updated" date above, and significant changes will be reflected in the app.
